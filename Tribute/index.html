Milestones

  • Apr. 1976:
    Apple founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne
  • Jul. 1976:
    "Apple 1", the company's first product launched
  • Jun. 1983:
    Apple Lisa released. It was one of the first computers with a GUI
  • Jan. 1984:
    Debut of the Macintosh personal computer.
  • Aug. 1998:
    iMac desktop computer unveiled
  • Nov. 2001:
    Apple introduces the iPod
  • Apr. 2003:
    iTunes Store Opens
  • Jun. 2007:
    Apple announces the iPhone
  • Jul. 2008:
    App Store added to allow iPhone users to download applications
